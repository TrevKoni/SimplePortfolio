About Me

Hello, I'm Trev Koni, a passionate and aspiring Software Engineer. I have a strong background in computer science and a genuine love for coding. I am currently enrolled in a computer science bachelor's program at Polis University, where I have been exposed to various programming languages and technologies.

My journey in software engineering began during my high school years, where I had the opportunity to learn and work at a company specializing in web development. There, I gained practical experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, MySQL, and PHP. This hands-on experience ignited my passion for creating dynamic and visually appealing websites.

Throughout my studies, I have deepened my understanding of software engineering principles and practices. In addition to mastering Java, in my Internship I have explored advanced concepts such as Git, Angular, TypeScript, and SpringBoot for Java, further enhancing my skills in backend development and API implementation. I have also gained exposure to databases such as MySQL and PostgreSQL.
I am continuously expanding my knowledge and challenging myself with new technologies. In my free time, I have experimented with Vite, React, React-Native, and Tailwind CSS, building small projects to improve my skills and explore modern development frameworks.

Collaboration and teamwork are a part of my approach to software engineering. I enjoy working with others to bring ideas to life and create innovative solutions. My attention to detail, problem-solving abilities, and dedication make me a reliable and efficient software engineer.
